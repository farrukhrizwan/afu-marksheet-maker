Consolidated Result Formulas

In Words:

For EACH SUBJECT separately:

Subject Weighted Marks = (Exam 1 marks ÷ Exam 1 total marks × Exam 1 weight) + (Exam 2 marks ÷ Exam 2 total marks × Exam 2 weight)

Numerical Application (Example W1=50, W2=50):

Subject: Mathematics (100 marks each exam)
Exam 1: 40 marks obtained, Exam 2: 45 marks obtained

Exam 1 Weighted = (40 ÷ 100 × 50) = 20
Exam 2 Weighted = (45 ÷ 100 × 50) = 22.5
Consolidated Weighted Marks = 20 + 22.5 = 42.5

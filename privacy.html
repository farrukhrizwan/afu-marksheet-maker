🛡️ Privacy Summary: This app operates 100% client-side. Your data never leaves your device.

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Local Data Processing

This application is built by AFU WEBS (managed by Farrukh Barlas) as a "Client-Side" tool. This means all processing, file conversions, or calculations happen exclusively within your browser's memory using JavaScript. We do not have a backend server to receive, store, or view your uploaded content.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII). We do not use cookies to track your behavior, and we do not maintain databases of user activity. Once the browser tab is closed, any temporary data held in memory is purged.

3. Third-Party Services

While the app logic is local, the website may be hosted on platforms (such as GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel). These providers may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, browser types) for security and maintenance. We do not control or have access to this data for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

Because your data is never transmitted to us, the security of your data depends on the security of your own device and browser. We recommend using updated browsers and secure hardware.

5. External Links

Our application may contain links to our official social media or websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

6. Contact Information
